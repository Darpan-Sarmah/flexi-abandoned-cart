## Key Differentiators

### Flexi Abandoned Cart Recovery

**Strengths:**
- Fully open-source and GPL-licensed — no locked features.
- Lightweight with no third-party service dependencies.
- Easy setup with sensible defaults.
- Dynamic coupon generation available in the free version.

**Weaknesses (compared to pro competitors):**
- No drag-and-drop email editor.
- No A/B testing functionality.
- No SMS recovery channel.

---

## Conclusion

Flexi Abandoned Cart Recovery is an excellent choice for store owners who want a **reliable, open-source, free solution** with a solid feature set. It provides cart tracking, automated emails, dynamic coupons, and analytics — all without a paid subscription.

Competing pro plugins charge $79–$199/year for features that are largely available for free in this plugin.
