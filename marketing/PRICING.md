# Pricing Strategy

## Flexi Abandoned Cart Recovery for WooCommerce

---

## Distribution Model

The plugin is released as **open-source software** under the GPL-2.0-or-later licence and is available free of charge on WordPress.org.

---

## Free Tier (WordPress.org)

**Price:** Free

**Includes:**
- All core features (cart tracking, email sequences, dynamic coupons, analytics dashboard)
- Community support via WordPress.org support forum
- Updates via WordPress.org

---

## Commercial / Premium Tier (Optional Future Extension)

A commercial premium extension or hosted service could offer additional value-added features, such as:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Advanced email design editor | Drag-and-drop visual email builder |
| A/B testing | Test different subject lines and content |
| SMS recovery | Send recovery messages via SMS |
| Conditional email sequences | Segment emails by cart value, product category, or customer type |
| Priority support | Dedicated email support with guaranteed response time |
| Licence-managed updates | Automatic updates delivered from a private update server |

---

## Recommended Pricing Benchmarks (Competitor Reference)

| Plugin | Pricing |
|--------|---------|
| Recover WooCommerce Cart Abandonment | Free (basic), $149+/yr (pro) |
| Abandoned Cart Lite for WooCommerce | Free (basic), $119+/yr (pro) |
| CartBounty | Free (basic), €49–€99/yr (pro) |

A competitive price point for a premium extension would be in the range of **$79–$149/year** per site.

---

## Notes

- All pricing decisions must comply with the GPL licence terms. Premium extensions must be sold as separate add-ons, not as modifications to the GPL core.
- If offering commercial support or hosting services, these are independent of the GPL licence obligations.
