# Product Description

## Flexi Abandoned Cart Recovery for WooCommerce

### Short Description (150 characters max)

Recover lost sales with automated abandoned cart emails and dynamic discount coupons for WooCommerce stores.

---

### Medium Description (for marketplace listings)

Flexi Abandoned Cart Recovery for WooCommerce helps online store owners recover lost revenue by automatically detecting abandoned carts and sending personalised follow-up emails to bring customers back.

When a shopper enters their email address at checkout but leaves without buying, the plugin captures their cart and triggers a configurable email sequence. You can optionally include dynamically generated discount coupons to incentivise purchase completion.

Track every recovery in the built-in analytics dashboard and monitor email open rates, click-throughs, and converted revenue.

### Full Description

#### Stop Losing Sales to Cart Abandonment

On average, 70% of online shopping carts are abandoned before checkout is complete. Flexi Abandoned Cart Recovery for WooCommerce helps you win back those customers automatically — without any manual effort.

#### How It Works

1. A customer adds items to their cart and starts the checkout process.
2. They enter their email address but leave the page before completing their order.
3. The plugin detects the abandonment and schedules a series of recovery emails.
4. Each email is fully customisable — include the cart contents, a personalised greeting, and an optional discount coupon.
5. The customer clicks the recovery link, returns to a pre-filled cart, and completes their purchase.
6. You see the recovered sale in the analytics dashboard.

#### Key Features

- **Real-Time Cart Tracking** – Captures email addresses and cart contents as soon as they are entered at checkout.
- **Automated Email Sequences** – Configure multiple follow-up emails at custom intervals (e.g. 1 hour, 24 hours, 72 hours).
- **Dynamic Coupon Generation** – Automatically create unique, single-use WooCommerce coupons for each recovery email.
- **Analytics Dashboard** – Monitor abandoned cart volume, recovery rate, and total revenue recovered at a glance.
- **Email Performance Metrics** – Track open rates, click-through rates, and conversion rates per email in the sequence.
- **Fully Customisable Templates** – Edit email subject lines and body content with support for dynamic variables.
- **WooCommerce Native** – Built on WooCommerce hooks and APIs for reliable, conflict-free integration.
- **Translation Ready** – Ships with a `.pot` translation template so you can localise the plugin into any language.
- **GDPR Friendly** – Only tracks customers who have voluntarily provided their email address.

#### Who Is This For?

- WooCommerce store owners who want to recover lost revenue without complex marketing automation tools.
- Developers building or maintaining WooCommerce stores for clients.
- Growing e-commerce businesses looking for a lightweight, reliable cart recovery solution.

#### Requirements

- WordPress 5.9+
- WooCommerce 6.0+
- PHP 7.4+
